The airbus a380

The airbus a380 is a large wide body passenger aircraft.

The Airbus A380 is the world's largest passenger airliner and only full length double deck jet aircraft. Capable of carrying up to 853 passengers, this four engine wide body aircraft revolutionized long haul trvel with its spacious cabins, quiet engines, smooth ride.
